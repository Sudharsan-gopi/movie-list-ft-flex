My Favourite Movies list

7G Rainbow Colony

It’s not just a love story—it’s a heartbreakingly honest look at transformation, yearning, and the power of connection.

Scent Of A Woman

It’s a poignant reminder that even the most broken souls can teach us how to live with honor.

Good Will Hunting

Good Will Hunting is a deeply affecting exploration of genius, trauma, and the transformative power of human connection.

IKIRU

Ikiru is a profoundly moving portrait of finding purpose in the face of death.

Johnny

Rajinikanth’s haunting dual performance in a poetic tale of identity, love, and redemption.

Virumandi

Virumaandi is a gripping, Rashomon-style tale of justice, vengeance, and the human cost of capital punishment, anchored by Kamal Haasan’s powerhouse performance.

500 Days Of Summer

500 Days of Summer is a bittersweet, non-linear journey through love, heartbreak, and the illusions we create around relationships.

12 Angry Men

12 Angry Men is a riveting, claustrophobic drama that brilliantly dissects justice, prejudice, and the power of one dissenting voice.
